What is Aflatoxin?

Aflatoxin, a carcinogen, is produced in food infected by the fungus, Aspergillus. It is mostly found in maize, peanuts and chillies. Ingesting small quantities of the toxins over the long term can cause liver or at times lung cancer. (infographic with stats)
